What is mindful running?

Running can sometimes provide an outlet to disconnect, which can be necessary at times. Running mindfully is a decission to pay attention to certain things at certain times. For example: what you see, smell, hear around you.

It's about acknowledging your mind will wander and then gently bringing it back to the intended focus.

Give it a try!

  • Bring your body to a state of calm before you begin your run.

  • Run outside not on a treadmill.

  • Try some pre run mindful breathing.

  • Leave the headphones at home, engage all your senses.

  • Pay attention to your thoughts.

  • Start slow and listen to your body.

  • Take time to reflect when you finish.
